WARNING!

Anyone that has your Facebook.com (FB) cookie will have access to your Facebook account. That means they can do anything you can do with your account.

This site will not store your FB cookie on disk. It will only be used to authenticate to Tinder.

If you want to revoke the cookie that you give this site, go to the browser that you got the cookie from and logout/sign out of your FB session.

How to Get Your Facebook Cookie

  • Chrome:
    1. Open a new tab.
    2. Press "control+shift+i" to open Chrome Developer tools.
    3. Click the "Network" tab.
    4. Check the "Preserve log" and "Disable cahce" boxes.
    5. In the same tab, go to Facebook App authentication page
    6. Click on the line with the name "login_success.html". You should see somethng like this.
    7. Copy the string to the right of "cookie:" (It has been removed from the image above.), do not include the "cookie:" label.
    8. Paste this string (with out the double quotes) into the text field above.
    9. Click "Submit"
  • Firefox:
    1. Open a new tab.
    2. Press "control+shift+i" to open Firefox Developer tools.
    3. Click the "Network" tab.
    4. In the same tab, go to Facebook App authentication page
    5. Click on the line with the name "login_success.html". You should see somethng like this.
    6. Click on the line that says "cookie:" (It has been removed from the image above.), copy the text out of there.
    7. Paste this string (with out the double quotes) into the text field above.
    8. Click "Submit"
